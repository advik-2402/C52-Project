About

Oberoi International School is an International School in Mumbai, India, it was opened on 17 July 2008, and is directed by Bindu Oberoi, who has directed it since the school was started. The school is fully accredited for all the programmes it offers. It has now also received the Council of International Schools accreditation. Oberoi International School's second campus at JVLR commenced operations for Nursery to Grade 8 since the academic year of 2017-18.
